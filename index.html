Advice for Parents

Simple guidance to support your child’s development every day

Motor Skills

Encourage your child to move, explore, and play actively. Activities like crawling, running, drawing, and building help develop both fine and gross motor skills. Give them safe space to practice independence and confidence.

Communication

Talk to your child often, listen carefully, and respond with patience. Reading books together and asking simple questions helps improve language, confidence, and emotional expression.

Feeding

Create a calm and positive eating environment. Offer a variety of healthy foods and allow your child to explore textures and tastes. Avoid pressure — let them develop healthy habits naturally.

Sensory Development

Support sensory growth through play — textures, sounds, colors, and movement. Activities like sand play, water play, music, and art help children understand the world around them.
